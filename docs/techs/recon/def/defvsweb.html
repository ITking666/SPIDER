Comparison of defocus estimates: manual (Web) vs automated (def)

Defocus was estimated in rotationally averaged power spectra (roo*** files), first in Web, then by the program def. Results are compared below.

The automated program worked better on newer, higher quality data, so the results are split into two groups.

New data (40s, 43s, 70s, eft) were generally acquired with a FEG, scanned by ZI to 2.82 pixels/A.

Older data (etu, rrf, ryr, acn) used Lab6, HiScan, with 4.78 .. 5.18 pixels/A.
